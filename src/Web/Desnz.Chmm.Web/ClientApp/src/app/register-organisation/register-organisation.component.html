Register your organisation for a M-CHMM account

How do I register?

The following pages will ask you to provide the information listed below about your organisation. Once you submit this information, the Administrator will review your registration. They may contact you if they have any questions. If they are happy with the information you provide, they will approve your account. You will be informed of this and will be able to log into your account.
