Do you sell relevant fossil fuel boilers?

Fossil fuel boilers fired by natural gas, liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), or oil, with a capacity of no more than 70kWth, sold on the UK market are considered relevant boiler sales under the Clean Heat Market Mechanism. See the Scheme Guidance for more information about which fossil fuel boilers are relevant.
