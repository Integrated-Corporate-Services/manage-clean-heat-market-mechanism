Are you the main contact person for your organisation?

If you are a Scheme Participant or Near-threshold Supplier

The Clean Heat Market Mechanism policy requires that there is a named person associated with the organisation account who is responsible for receiving any formal notices of legal correspondence. This is known as the Senior Responsible Officer. You do not have to be the Senior Responsible Officer to complete this registration, but you need to provide the details of the person who is. See the Scheme Guidance for more information.

If you are not a Scheme Participant or Near-threshold Supplier

You need to provide the details of a named contact person for your organisation account. This will be the person who the Administrator makes contact with if there are any questions relating to the account.
