Clean Heat Market Mechanism account registration successfully submitted

What happens now?

The Clean Heat Market Mechanism Administrator has now received your registration. They will review the registration and may contact you to discuss or ask further questions. If you need to speak to the Administrator during this time, please contact chmm@environment-agency.gov.uk.

When the Administrator is satisfied, they will approve your account. You will recieve a notification of this and which includes details of how to access the Clean Heat Market Mechanism system.

Once your account has been activated, you will be able to add other users to the account.
